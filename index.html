Instructions on using the Semester Exchange Portal

  1. Click on the appropriate filter that you want to apply for your required course mappings
  2. Multiple filters can be stacked on top of each other for highly selective mappings
  3. The ‘Department’ field returns courses mapped by students of that department on previous semester exchanges
  4. Course descriptions and credits will be displayed once the user clicks on their required course mapping

**The provided course mappings have been verified and used by previous students on semester exchange. However, new mappings can also be discovered and used.
